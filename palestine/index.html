Palestine: Qur’aan, Sunnah, and the Way of the Salaf

In the name of Allah, the All-Merciful, the Especially Merciful.
All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds, and may peace and blessings be upon our Prophet Muhammad, his family, and his companions.
To proceed:
The Palestinian cause is not a national or partisan issue, nor is it merely a political conflict between opposing sides.
Rather, it is a religious, creedal matter, measured by the scale of the Qur'aan, the Sunnah, and the understanding of the Salaf of this Ummah.
No amount of slogans and chants has changed any reality, nor will that bring victory to a nation heedless of its Lord.
Allah, Exalted He is, says:
If you support Allah, He will support you
and He says:
It was ever incumbent upon Us to help the believers
Therefore, it is obligatory upon us to examine ourselves before casting blame on others; to rectify our creed, our worship, and our conduct; and to return to our religion in a true way.
When the inward is set aright, the outward becomes upright. And when the Ummah is truthful with its Lord, the promise of Allah for victory and establishment will come to pass.
But if sins persist, and shirk and innovations spread, and trusts are lost, and obligations are abandoned, then it is no wonder that our enemies are set upon us. The Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, said:
When you make engaging in ‘Inah transactions a habit, take hold of the tails of cattle, are pleased with farming, and abandon jihaad, Allah will inflict humiliation upon you, and He will not remove it until you return to your religion.
Humiliation is only lifted by a sincere return to religion.
We ask Allah to set Muslims aright, to grant victory to the oppressed in Palestine and elsewhere.
Abdurrahmaan Meehoob al-Qaddaaree 2025-07-30 11:10

The bankrupt Ikhwaan group published an article (20 Muharram 1447) in which it said:

In this particular phase, the Muslim Brotherhood is facing a chaotic, all-out-war involving the Zionists, the secularists, the Shiites, the atheists, the Madkhalis, and various factions and groups that had never confronted anyone before — yet in this phase they have spoken out and took aim on the Brotherhood. But we are confident that Allah is with us.

I say: this is turning the truth on its head. It is Ahl al-Sunnah who are truly confronting the disbelievers and the people of innovation. No one has exposed and clarified the danger of the Shiites, secularists, atheists, and Jews like Ahl al-Sunnah have.

As for the bankrupt Ikhwaan, they cooperate with anyone who supports them and serves their party — be they Christians, Shiites, or others — and they accept into their ranks all the deviant sects, including the Sufis, Jahmiyyah, and Ash'aris.

They have strong ties with the Rafidah of Iran, visit them, praise and thank the Houthis and Abd al-Malik al-Hoothee. Before that, they praised Hizbullah and prayed for Ḥasan Zumeirah [(Nasrallah)], and they even pray for some Christians.

Secularists sit with them and they with the secularists. The clearest example of that is Turkey.

The truth is, the bankrupt Ikhwaan are the ones who conspired with the Shiites of Iran and the Jews against the people of Gaza and others. Most of the revolutions in Muslim lands — they had a hand in them.

We ask Allah, Exalted is He, to protect the people of Gaza from everyone who has deceived them or caused war upon them.

As for their statement:

But we are confident that Allah is with us."

These are nothing but empty wishes. Allah, Exalted is He, said:

Indeed, Allah is with those who fear Him and those who do good. [an-Nahl: 128].

You — O bankrupt Ikhwaan — are neither of those who fear Him nor of those who do good. Rather, you are misguided innovators, from the sects of the Khawarij. May Allah suffice us against your evil.

—Abu al-Abbaas Muhammad al-Qasraaawee
